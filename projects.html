Circleshelf LLC

Circleshelf is a peer-to-peer online textbook marketplace that
allows students to connect and share the books they need.

The web app allows for safe and efficient transactions and does
away with creepy Craiglist meet ups and Facebook back-and-forth.

If you have a textbook, recoup some of the high costs. If you need a
textbook, browse the entire whole campus for it.
